Station-Searching

Original Music and Animated Visuals for Audiovisual Performance at Curiosity Shop: Hidden Meadow 2021, Streamed Live on Twitch. (Saturday 2021 05 29)

Includes Remix of "Hinagiku" by Gutevolk.



Selected Frames:


Character

The short originally featured a character I designed, modelled, textured and rigged, animated using motion capture. I completed it to the stage of getting motion capture working however was unhappy with the quality of the result. In the end I was unable to use it in the finished short due to time constraints but it was a fun learning experience to create.
