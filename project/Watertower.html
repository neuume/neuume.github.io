Watertower Stickers

Inspired by our mutual love of landscape-scale vessels for Water, a set of animated Stickers available on LINE produced in collaboration with Osa Uveine together as Objects on Parade.

Towers were designed though a process of trading the 3D models for eachother to edit and add details to, Then rotated and rendered by Osa.
